# Copyright in the Netherlands

Copyright in the Netherlands is governed by the Dutch Copyright Act known as [_Auteurswet_](https://wetten.overheid.nl/BWBR0001886/2026-01-01)

## Dutch Copyright Landscape 
### Copyright Law 
- What is protected: Original works that are perceptible to the senses (e.g., can be seen, read, or heard)
- How is copyright applied: Works are automatically protected if eligible for copyright protection
- Lenght of protection: Creator's life plus 70 years
 
### Public Norms

## Creative Commons in the Netherlands
- The Netherlands have their own CC chapter which is supported by the [Open Netherlands Association](https://www.opennederland.nl/)
- The Open Netherlands Association, a network of organizations and professionals (including Code for NL, Creative Commons Netherlands, and Wikimedia Netherlands) connects different sectors of open access supporters and promotes access to materials that can be freely used, edited, and shared.
